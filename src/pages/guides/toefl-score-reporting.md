# TOEFL Score Reporting: Everything You Need to Know

Once the exam is over, the next step is getting your scores to your target universities. Here is a guide to the reporting process.

## When Will I Get My Scores?

*   **Unofficial Scores:** You will see your unofficial Reading and Listening scores immediately after the test.
*   **Official Scores:** Your full score report (including Speaking and Writing) will be available in your ETS account about 4-8 days after the test.

## Sending Scores to Universities

1.  **Free Score Reports:** You can select up to four universities to receive your scores for free *before* you take the test.
2.  **Additional Reports:** After the test, you can send scores to more institutions for a fee (typically around $25 per report).
3.  **Delivery Time:** It can take 1-2 weeks for universities to receive your official scores after you request them.

## How Long Are Scores Valid?

TOEFL scores are valid for **two years** from the date of the exam. After two years, they are removed from your account and can no longer be reported.

## Summary

Plan your test date at least 2-3 months before your university application deadlines to ensure your scores arrive on time!
