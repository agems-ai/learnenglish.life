# 7 Best TOEFL Online Courses for 2026

Preparing for the TOEFL requires a structured approach. While self-study is possible, a high-quality online course can provide the strategies and feedback needed to hit your target score.

## Top Picks for 2026

1. **Magoosh:** Best for overall value and accessibility.
2. **BestMyTest:** Best for extensive practice questions and mock tests.
3. **Kaplan:** Best for students who want a more traditional, intensive classroom feel.
4. **ETS Official Prep:** Best for authentic practice material directly from the test makers.
5. **PrepScholar:** Best for personalized study plans.
6. **Udemy (various instructors):** Best for budget-conscious learners looking for specific skill focus.
7. **EDX (TOEFL Test Preparation):** Best free option for foundational strategies.

## What to Look for in a Course

*   **Authentic Practice:** Does the course use questions that mimic the actual TOEFL iBT?
*   **Speaking/Writing Feedback:** Will your responses be graded by real people or advanced AI?
*   **Score Guarantee:** Does the platform offer a refund or extra support if your score doesn't improve?

## Final Verdict

If you're on a budget, **Magoosh** is hard to beat. If you need maximum practice and a higher score guarantee, **BestMyTest** is our top recommendation.
