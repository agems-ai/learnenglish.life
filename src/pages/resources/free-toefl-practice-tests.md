# Where to Find the Best Free TOEFL Practice Tests

Don't spend a fortune on prep materials before checking out these high-quality free resources. Practice tests are the single most effective way to prepare for the exam.

## Top 3 Free Resources

1.  **ETS Free Practice Test:** The official test makers offer a full-length practice test that uses past exam questions. This is the most accurate resource available.
2.  **Magoosh TOEFL Flashcards & Trials:** While their full course is paid, Magoosh offers excellent free flashcards and a limited-time trial that includes practice questions.
3.  **Test-Guide:** Offers several free practice quizzes for Reading, Listening, and Structure.

## Why Practice Tests Matter

*   **Familiarity:** You'll learn the exact instructions for each section, saving you time on test day.
*   **Pacing:** You'll learn how to manage your time so you don't leave questions unanswered.
*   **Weakness Identification:** You'll see exactly which areas (like Speaking or Reading) need more of your attention.

## Pro Tip

Always take at least one practice test in a single sitting to build the stamina required for the actual 2-hour exam.
