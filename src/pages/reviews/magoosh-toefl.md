# Magoosh TOEFL Review 2026: Is It the Best Value?

Magoosh has built a reputation for providing high-quality test prep at a fraction of the cost of traditional competitors. In this review, we look at whether their TOEFL course lives up to the hype.

## Pros & Cons

### Pros
*   **Affordability:** One of the most budget-friendly options on the market.
*   **User Interface:** Clean, intuitive, and easy to navigate.
*   **Video Lessons:** Every practice question comes with a video explanation.
*   **Mobile App:** Study on the go with their excellent mobile app.

### Cons
*   **Fewer Practice Tests:** They offer fewer full-length mock exams than BestMyTest.
*   **Email Support Only:** There is no live chat or phone support for students.

## Pricing

Magoosh typically offers two plans:
*   **1 Month:** Around $109
*   **6 Months:** Around $129 (Best Value)

## Verdict

If you are a self-motivated learner looking for a clear, effective, and affordable way to prepare for the TOEFL, **Magoosh is our top recommendation.** The value for money is simply unbeatable.
