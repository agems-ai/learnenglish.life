Detailed Platform Reviews

1. iTalki — Best Overall

iTalki offers the best combination of price flexibility and tutor variety. Whether you want casual conversation practice or structured lessons, you can find it here. Starting prices from $5/hour make it accessible for learners on any budget.

Rating: 9.2/10

2. Preply — Best for Beginners

Preply provides a structured learning path with homework, progress tracking, and curriculum-based lessons. Perfect for beginners who need guidance and accountability.

Rating: 8.8/10

3. Cambly — Best Budget Option

Cambly's subscription model ($9.99/month) offers unlimited conversation practice with native speakers. Ideal for learners who want to practice speaking frequently without watching costs.

Rating: 8.0/10

4. Verbling — Best for Professional Tutors

Verbling focuses on professional, vetted tutors with structured lesson plans. Higher prices but more consistent quality and professionalism.

Rating: 8.4/10

5. Lingoda — Best Group Classes

Lingoda offers small group classes and corporate training. More expensive per lesson but includes group interaction and structured curriculum.

Rating: 7.8/10

How to Choose

Consider these factors when choosing a platform:

  • Budget: iTalki and Cambly offer the lowest prices
  • Learning style: Preply for structure, iTalki for flexibility
  • Goals: Conversation practice vs. exam prep vs. business English
  • Schedule: Some platforms require booking in advance
