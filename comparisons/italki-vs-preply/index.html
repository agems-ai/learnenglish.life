What is iTalki?

iTalki is the world's largest language tutoring marketplace. It connects learners with over 10,000 tutors from 100+ countries. iTalki offers two types of tutors:

  • Community tutors: Native speakers who teach casually. Prices start at $5/hour. No formal teaching qualifications required.
  • Professional tutors: Qualified teachers with formal training. Prices typically $15-$50/hour.

iTalki is known for its flexibility. You book lessons directly with tutors, choose your own schedule, and can switch tutors easily.

What is Preply?

Preply is also a tutoring marketplace, but with a stronger focus on structured learning. It offers a built-in curriculum and trial lessons before committing to a tutor.

Preply categorizes tutors into:

  • Community tutors: Similar to iTalki, native speakers teaching casually.
  • Certified tutors: Tutors with verified teaching certificates (TEFL, CELTA, etc.).

Preply's trial lesson feature ($2-5 for 20 minutes) lets you test a tutor's teaching style before booking regular sessions.

Pricing Comparison

Both platforms have wide price ranges depending on tutor type and experience.

iTalki Pricing

  • Community tutors: $5-$15/hour
  • Professional tutors: $15-$50/hour
  • Trial lessons: Free (community) or paid (professional)
  • Lesson packages: Buy 5+ lessons for 5-15% discount

Preply Pricing

  • Community tutors: $10-$25/hour
  • Certified tutors: $25-$80/hour
  • Trial lessons: ~$2-5 for 20 minutes
  • Lesson packages: Discounts for bulk purchases

Winner: iTalki for budget learners. Community tutors start at $5/hour, compared to Preply's $10/hour minimum.

Tutor Quality

iTalki: Quality varies significantly between community and professional tutors. Community tutors may have excellent English but limited teaching skills. Professional tutors have verified qualifications but cost more.

Preply: Similar quality variance, but Preply's "Certified" filter makes it easier to find qualified teachers. The trial lesson system helps you verify quality before committing.

Winner: Tie. Both platforms require careful tutor selection. Use reviews and trial lessons on either platform.

Features Comparison

iTalki extras:

  • iTalki Notebook — built-in vocabulary builder
  • Community conversations — free group speaking practice
  • Instant tutoring — book lessons with available tutors immediately
  • Flexible scheduling — tutors set their own availability

Preply extras:

  • Learning path — structured curriculum for goal-oriented learners
  • Trial lessons — test before you commit
  • Progress tracking — monitor your improvement over time
  • Corporate plans — team learning for businesses

User Experience

iTalki: Clean, functional interface. Filter tutors by price, rating, availability, and specialty. Video calls use integrated Zoom-style technology. The mobile apps work well for booking and rescheduling.

Preply: Modern, polished interface. Better built-in learning materials. The "find your perfect tutor" quiz helps new users narrow down options. Slightly smoother user experience overall.

Who Should Choose iTalki?

  • Budget learners: Community tutors from $5/hour
  • Flexible schedules: Need evening or weekend lessons
  • Casual practice: Want conversation without strict curriculum
  • Instant lessons: Can't plan ahead, need on-demand availability
  • Variety seekers: Want to try many different tutors

Who Should Choose Preply?

  • Goal-oriented learners: Want a structured path with progress tracking
  • Careful choosers: Prefer to test tutors via trial lessons first
  • Quality-first learners: Willing to pay more for certified teachers
  • Corporate users: Need team learning plans for employees
  • Beginners: Want guidance on which tutor level to start with

Final Verdict

iTalki wins for: Budget learners, flexible scheduling, and casual conversation practice. The $5/hour starting price makes quality tutoring accessible to everyone.

Preply wins for: Structured learners who want curriculum guidance, trial lessons, and certified teacher verification.

Both platforms are legitimate and have helped millions of learners improve their English. The "right" choice depends on your budget, learning style, and goals. If you're unsure, start with iTalki's free community tutors to get comfortable with online lessons, then upgrade to professional tutors when you're ready.
