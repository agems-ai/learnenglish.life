Detailed Analysis

Preply Overview

Preply emphasizes structured learning with a curriculum-based approach. Each tutor provides lesson plans, homework, and progress reports. This makes it ideal for learners who need clear goals and accountability in their English journey.

Preply tutors typically teach specific skills: conversation, grammar, business English, or exam preparation (IELTS, TOEFL). Lessons are scheduled in advance and require commitment, but you get professional instruction rather than casual chat.

All Preply tutors go through an application process including video interviews and teaching demonstrations, ensuring consistent quality and professional qualifications like TEFL or CELTA certificates.

Cambly Overview

Cambly offers unlimited conversation practice with native English speakers through a simple subscription model. There's no curriculum, no homework, and no pressure. You talk when you want, about what you want.

At just $9.99/month, Cambly provides exceptional value for learners who want maximum speaking practice without the commitment of formal lessons. Connect instantly with tutors from around the world—no advance booking required.

This makes Cambly perfect for intermediate+ learners who want to practice speaking without the structure of formal lessons. It's also the most budget-friendly option for heavy practice sessions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Preply or Cambly better for beginners?

Preply is better for beginners due to its structured curriculum, vetted professional tutors, and personalized learning plans. Cambly is ideal for intermediate+ learners who want conversation practice without formal lessons.

How much does each platform cost?

Cambly costs $9.99/month for unlimited conversation practice. Preply charges $10-45 per hour depending on tutor experience and credentials.

Can I get homework and track progress on Cambly?

No, Cambly offers conversation practice only with no curriculum, homework, or formal progress tracking. Preply provides structured lessons with homework and progress reports.

Which is better for budget learners?

Cambly is significantly better for budget learners. At $9.99/month for unlimited practice, it costs less than $2/hour equivalent if you practice 5+ hours monthly. Preply charges $10+ per session.
