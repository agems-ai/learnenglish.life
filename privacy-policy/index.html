LearnEnglish.Life ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website. Please read this privacy policy carefully.

1 Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Email Address: When you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address. This is the only personal data field we require.
  • User-Submitted Content: Reviews, comments, ratings, and feedback you submit become publicly visible content associated with your display name or "Anonymous."
  • Communication Data: If you contact us via email or our contact form, we may collect the content of your message and contact information.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain technical information:

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and device type.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, clicks, referring URLs, and navigation patterns.
  • Location Data: General geographic location inferred from IP address (city/region level only). We do not collect precise GPS location.
  • Cookie Data: Information stored in cookies as described in Section 5.

1.3 Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Full IP addresses (we store only truncated/obfuscated versions)
  • Precise geolocation data
  • Social security numbers or national ID numbers
  • Financial or payment information (all payments are processed by third parties)
  • Health or biometric data
  • Passwords or authentication credentials

2 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide, maintain, and improve our website and services.
  • Newsletter Delivery: To send newsletters and updates you have subscribed to (you can unsubscribe anytime).
  • Analytics: To understand how visitors use our site and identify areas for improvement.
  • Advertising: To display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense.
  • Security: To protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal requests.
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.

3 Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your personal data based on the following legal grounds under GDPR Article 6:

  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)): For newsletter subscriptions and non-essential cookies. You can withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legitimate Interests (Art. 6(1)(f)): For essential cookies, analytics, and security. We have conducted a balancing test to ensure our interests do not override your rights.
  • Contractual Necessity (Art. 6(1)(b)): For processing required to provide our service to you.
  • Legal Obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)): For compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

6 Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Email Subscriptions: Retained until you unsubscribe. After unsubscription, your email is deleted within 30 days.
  • Analytics Data: Retained for 26 months in Google Analytics (standard retention period).
  • Server Logs: Retained for 30 days for security purposes.
  • User-Submitted Content: Reviews and comments are retained indefinitely as they become part of our public content. You may request removal per Section 8.
  • Communication Records: Retained for 2 years after last contact.

7 Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted to/from our site is encrypted via HTTPS (TLS 1.2+).
  • Secure Hosting: Our servers are hosted by DigitalOcean with industry-standard security.
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls limit who can access personal data.
  • Regular Updates: Software and systems are regularly updated to patch security vulnerabilities.
  • Security Monitoring: Cloudflare provides DDoS protection and threat detection.

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

8 Your Rights — General

Regardless of your location, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements).
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time (click "Unsubscribe" in any email).
  • Object: Object to certain processing of your data.
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
  • Lodge Complaint: File a complaint with a data protection authority if you believe your rights have been violated.

9 GDPR-Specific Rights (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

GDPR Articles 15-22 — Your Rights

  • Art. 15 — Right of Access: Obtain confirmation of whether we process your data and access to that data.
  • Art. 16 — Right to Rectification: Have inaccurate personal data corrected.
  • Art. 17 — Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Art. 18 — Right to Restriction: Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances.
  • Art. 19 — Notification: Receive notification of corrections, deletions, or restrictions.
  • Art. 20 — Right to Portability: Receive your data in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Art. 21 — Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests or public task.
  • Art. 22 — Automated Decisions: Not be subject to solely automated decision-making with legal effects.

Supervisory Authority: You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. For contact information, visit: edpb.europa.eu/members

Data Protection Officer: As a small-scale processor, we are not required to appoint a DPO. However, you can contact us at privacy@learnenglish.life for all privacy matters.

10 CCPA Rights (California Residents)

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

Your California Rights

  • Right to Know: Request disclosure of personal information collected, used, disclosed, or sold about you.
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information we have collected (subject to exceptions).
  • Right to Correct: Request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Not be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights.
  • Right to Limit Use: Limit use of sensitive personal information to that which is necessary.

Categories of Personal Information We Collect

In the past 12 months, we have collected:

  • Identifiers: Email address (only if you subscribe)
  • Internet Activity: Browsing history, interaction data, referring URLs
  • Geolocation Data: City/region level only (from IP address)
  • Inferences: Preferences and interests derived from browsing behavior

Do Not Sell or Share Personal Information

We do NOT sell personal information in the traditional sense. However, we use advertising cookies that may be considered "sharing" under CCPA. You can opt out of this by:

  • Rejecting non-essential cookies when visiting our site
  • Using the "Cookie Settings" link in our footer
  • Installing the Digital Advertising Alliance Opt-Out
