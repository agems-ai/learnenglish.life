What is ELSA Speak?

ELSA Speak (English Language Speech Assistant) is an AI-powered pronunciation app designed specifically for English learners. Unlike general language apps, ELSA focuses exclusively on helping you sound more native-like.

The app uses proprietary speech recognition technology trained on thousands of hours of English audio. It analyzes your pronunciation, intonation, rhythm, and stress patterns, then provides detailed feedback to help you improve.

ELSA was developed with support from Stanford University and is particularly popular among non-native speakers preparing for international exams (IELTS, TOEFL), job interviews, or business presentations.

Key Features

1. AI Pronunciation Analysis

ELSA's AI analyzes 23 aspects of English pronunciation including individual sounds, word stress, sentence rhythm, and intonation patterns. You get a detailed score and specific feedback for improvement.

2. Real-Time Feedback

Speak into your phone and receive instant feedback. ELSA highlights problem areas with visual waveforms and explains exactly what to fix.

3. Custom Learning Path

Take an initial assessment to identify your accent challenges. ELSA creates a personalized curriculum focusing on your specific problem areas rather than wasting time on sounds you already master.

4. Exam Preparation Modules

Special modules for IELTS and TOEFL speaking preparation with practice questions and scoring aligned to exam criteria.

5. Business English

Practice professional vocabulary, presentations, and interview skills with role-play scenarios and industry-specific vocabulary.

6. Progress Tracking

Track your improvement over time with detailed analytics showing your pronunciation score evolution across all areas.

Our Verdict

ELSA Speak is the best app specifically for pronunciation improvement. If you struggle with sounding more native-like, ELSA's AI analysis provides insights you can't get from any other app.

However, ELSA is not a complete English learning solution. It focuses only on speaking and pronunciation. Use it alongside apps like Duolingo or Busuu for vocabulary and grammar.

ELSA is best for:

  • Intermediate learners focusing on pronunciation
  • Exam takers preparing for speaking tests
  • Professionals improving business English
  • Anyone wanting to reduce their accent
