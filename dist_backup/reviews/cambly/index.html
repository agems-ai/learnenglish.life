What Is Cambly?

Cambly is an online English tutoring platform that connects learners with native English speakers for on-demand conversation practice. Founded in 2012 and based in San Francisco, Cambly differentiates itself from traditional tutoring platforms by offering unlimited practice sessions for a flat monthly or annual subscription fee.

Unlike platforms where you book specific lesson times with a specific tutor, Cambly operates more like a video chat service: you log in, tap a button, and get connected to an available native speaker within seconds. It is designed for learners who want flexible, low-pressure conversation practice without the commitment of scheduling.

How Does Cambly Work?

Using Cambly is refreshingly simple:

  1. Sign up and choose a plan. Pick between monthly or annual subscription. The annual plan offers significant savings.
  2. Set your preferences. Choose your dialect preference (American, British, or Australian English), and set your proficiency level.
  3. Start a conversation. Tap the "Start Speaking" button. Cambly connects you with an available tutor within moments.
  4. Talk for as long as you want. There is no time limit per session. You can speak for 5 minutes or 2 hours.
  5. Save tutors you like. If you have a great conversation with someone, you can save them as a favorite and request them in future sessions.

Pricing

Cambly uses a straightforward subscription model. There are no per-minute charges or lesson credits to manage.

Tutor Availability

Cambly's tutors are all native English speakers, which is a major selling point. The platform employs two types of tutors:

  • Community tutors. Native English speakers from the US, UK, Canada, Australia, and other English-speaking countries. These are often university students, retired professionals, or language enthusiasts who tutor in their spare time.
  • Academic tutors. A smaller group of tutors with formal teaching credentials. These tutors offer more structured sessions and may help with academic writing, exam prep, or business English.

Wait times vary depending on the time of day. During peak hours (morning and evening in North America), you can usually connect within 30 seconds. During off-peak times, you might wait two to five minutes. Tutors from different time zones help extend availability, but the best coverage is during North American business hours.

The Conversation-Only Model

This is the most important thing to understand about Cambly: it is purely conversation-based. There is no built-in curriculum, no grammar lessons, no vocabulary drills, and no structured homework. You simply talk.

For some learners, this is exactly what they need. Speaking practice with native speakers is one of the fastest ways to improve pronunciation, build confidence, and develop natural language patterns. The lack of structure also means there is no pressure and no homework.

However, if you need structured learning, grammar explanations, or a clear progression path, Cambly alone will not provide that. You will need to combine it with other resources or look for a platform that includes a curriculum.

Kids and Family Subscriptions

Cambly offers a dedicated kids version called Cambly Kids, which is designed for children aged 4 to 14. On this version, tutors use more visual aids, games, and age-appropriate conversation topics. The platform also has stricter tutor vetting for the kids section.

For families, Cambly Family plans let multiple children share one subscription, which makes it more affordable for households with multiple English learners.

Platform Features

  • On-demand access. Connect to a native speaker whenever you want, no scheduling needed.
  • Unlimited conversations. No time limits per session or per month.
  • Topic selection. Before connecting, you can choose a topic (travel, business, daily life) to help guide the conversation.
  • Save favorite tutors. Build relationships with tutors you enjoy talking with.
  • Session recordings. Review your conversations later to revisit vocabulary and phrases.
  • Dialect preference. Choose American, British, or Australian English.

Verdict

Cambly is an excellent tool for one specific purpose: getting more spoken English practice with native speakers. If your main goal is to become more comfortable speaking English and you want unlimited practice without the pressure of scheduling or paying per session, Cambly delivers exactly that.

The annual plan is genuinely good value at around $8 per month for unlimited native-speaker conversation time. That is hard to beat compared to per-session tutoring rates that can run $20 to $60 per hour.

However, Cambly is not a complete English learning solution. It does not teach grammar, vocabulary systematically, or prepare you for specific exams. Think of it as an excellent supplement to a structured learning program rather than a standalone course.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Cambly good for beginners?

Yes, Cambly can work for beginners, though you may find conversations challenging at first. The informal nature of Cambly sessions means tutors are used to working with learners at all levels. Start with shorter sessions and work up to longer conversations as your confidence grows.

Can I choose a specific tutor on Cambly?

You can save tutors as favorites and request them when they are online, but you cannot schedule with a specific tutor in advance. If you want guaranteed sessions with a specific teacher, Cambly may not be the right platform for you.

Does Cambly have grammar lessons?

No. Cambly is a conversation-only platform. Tutors can help correct your grammar in conversation, but there are no dedicated grammar lessons, vocabulary units, or structured courses built into the platform.

Is Cambly better than iTalki or Preply?

It depends on your goals. Cambly is cheaper for unlimited speaking practice but offers no structure. iTalki and Preply give you access to certified teachers with formal lesson plans. If you want structured learning with a dedicated teacher, those platforms may be better. If you want affordable, unlimited speaking practice, Cambly wins.

Can I cancel my Cambly subscription?

Yes. Cambly offers a 7-day money-back guarantee on first-time subscriptions. After that, you can cancel anytime, but annual plan refunds are prorated based on unused months.
