What Is Preply?

Preply is an online language tutoring marketplace that connects learners with tutors from around the world for live, 1-on-1 video lessons. Founded in 2012 and headquartered in Austin, Texas, the platform has grown to feature more than 100,000 tutors teaching over 50 languages.

Unlike platforms where you are assigned a tutor, Preply lets you browse profiles, read reviews, watch intro videos, and book lessons with whoever fits your learning style. You can take lessons anytime, day or night, because tutors span every time zone.

Preply is particularly popular among English learners because it offers tutors from native English-speaking countries as well as non-native tutors who speak your language. This makes it easier to find a tutor who understands your specific challenges.

How Does Preply Work?

Getting started with Preply is straightforward. You create a free account, set your language level, and start browsing tutors. Each tutor profile shows their hourly rate, availability, specializations, student reviews, and a short intro video.

Once you find a tutor you like, you can book a trial lesson at a discounted rate (usually around $5-$20 depending on the tutor). This gives you a chance to see if their teaching style works for you before committing to regular lessons.

Lessons take place on Preply own own video platform, which includes a virtual whiteboard, screen sharing, and the ability to upload materials. You can also reschedule lessons up to 24 hours before the session, which gives you flexibility if your schedule changes.

Preply Pricing

Preply does not use a subscription model. Instead, you pay per lesson, and prices vary significantly depending on the tutor you choose. Here is a general breakdown of what to expect:

Tutor Tier Hourly Rate Best For
Community Tutors $5 - $15/hr Conversation practice, budget learners
Certified Tutors $15 - $40/hr Structured lessons, exam prep
Expert Tutors $40 - $100+/hr Business English, advanced learners

Most learners on Preply work with certified tutors in the $15-$30/hour range. You can also buy lesson packages of 10 or 20 sessions for a small discount, which can bring the effective hourly rate down by 5-15%.

One thing to note: if you cancel your subscription or stop lessons, any unused credits stay in your account. There is no monthly minimum commitment, which makes Preply more flexible than subscription-based platforms like Cambly.

Teacher Quality

Teacher quality on Preply varies more than on platforms like Cambly or iTalki, where tutors go through a more formal vetting process. Preply allows tutors to set up profiles directly, which means anyone can theoretically offer lessons.

That said, Preply does have quality signals built into the platform. Tutors with higher ratings and more completed lessons appear higher in search results. Look for tutors with at least 50 completed lessons and a 4.8-star rating or higher before making your decision.

Certified tutors on Preply have completed Preply internal training program and passed a teaching certification. These tutors typically charge more but tend to deliver more structured lessons with clear learning plans.

Key Features

Preply Tutor Marketplace

Preply gives you access to over 100,000 tutors. You can filter by native language, accent preference, specializations (business English, IELTS prep, conversation practice), and availability. This variety means you are more likely to find a tutor who matches your specific needs.

Trial Lessons

Most tutors on Preply offer a trial lesson at a reduced rate. This is a great way to test whether a tutor is the right fit before committing to a full lesson package. We recommend trying at least two or three tutors before settling on one.

Preply Spaces

Preply Spaces is a virtual classroom feature that lets you practice writing, use interactive exercises, and review lesson recordings. It is available on the web and mobile app, so you can continue learning even between live sessions.

Corporate Training

For businesses, Preply offers team plans with dedicated account managers, progress tracking, and group sessions. This makes it a viable option for companies looking to upskill their employees in English.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Preply good for beginners?

Yes. Preply is suitable for beginners, but look for tutors who specialize in teaching beginners or hold a teaching certification. Community tutors at the lower price range may not have the skills to help a complete beginner effectively.

How much does Preply cost per month?

Preply does not require a monthly subscription. You pay per lesson, with prices ranging from $5 to over $100 per hour depending on the tutor. If you take two lessons per week at $20 per session, you can expect to spend around $160 per month.

Can I change tutors on Preply?

Yes. You can book lessons with as many different tutors as you want. Many learners work with two or three tutors simultaneously, using one for conversation practice and another for structured grammar lessons.

Does Preply offer a free trial?

Preply does not offer a platform-wide free trial. However, individual tutors set their own trial lesson prices, and many offer trial lessons for $5 to $20. This gives you a low-cost way to test the platform.

How is Preply different from iTalki?

Both platforms connect you with tutors for 1-on-1 lessons. The main difference is that Preply offers a wider range of tutor prices and does not require a monthly subscription, while iTalki has a more structured community tutor option. For a full comparison, see our Preply vs iTalki guide.
