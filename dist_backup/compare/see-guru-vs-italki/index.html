Detailed Analysis

Pricing and Value

Both platforms offer lessons starting at $5/hour, making them accessible for budget-conscious learners. However, See Guru often provides lower average prices ($10-20/hr) compared to iTalki ($15-25/hr). Trial lessons are notably cheaper on See Guru ($1-5) versus iTalki ($5-15).

iTalki's higher average price reflects its larger pool of certified professional teachers. See Guru offers better value for learners specifically seeking Asian tutors at competitive rates.

Tutor Selection and Quality

iTalki dominates in scale with 10,000+ tutors including certified professional teachers, community tutors, and native English speakers. Their lesson replay feature and established reputation with 10M+ learners provide confidence in quality.

See Guru specializes in Asian tutors from China, Japan, and Korea. While the pool is smaller, the regional expertise and cultural understanding make it ideal for learners seeking native speakers from these regions at competitive prices.

User Experience

iTalki offers superior mobile apps (iOS & Android), flexible instant booking, and lesson replay. Their established platform provides stability and extensive user reviews to inform decisions.

See Guru provides a cleaner, more focused interface for Asian tutor search. Video introductions on all tutor profiles help learners make informed decisions. Both platforms require no subscription.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is See Guru or iTalki better for finding Asian tutors?

See Guru is specifically strong for Asian tutors from China, Japan, and Korea, often at lower price points. iTalki has a broader global selection but See Guru excels in regional expertise and cultural understanding.

How much does a lesson cost on See Guru vs iTalki?

Both platforms start at $5/hour for community tutors. However, See Guru typically offers lower average prices ($10-20/hr) compared to iTalki ($15-25/hr). Trial lessons are also more affordable on See Guru ($1-5 vs $5-15 on iTalki).

Which platform has better tutor quality?

iTalki has more robust vetting and professional certification options, with lesson replay features. See Guru has basic vetting but excels in Asian tutor specialization. Quality ultimately depends on individual tutors rather than the platform.

Can I switch between See Guru and iTalki?

Yes, both platforms work independently. Many learners use both: See Guru for Asian tutors and budget lessons, iTalki for broader tutor selection and professional instructors. No subscription is required on either platform.
