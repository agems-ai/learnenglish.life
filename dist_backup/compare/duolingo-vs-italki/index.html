What Are the Main Differences?

Duolingo is a language learning app built around daily gamified lessons. Each session takes five to ten minutes and covers vocabulary, grammar, listening, and reading through games, streaks, and rewards. It is free, fun, and highly addictive. Millions of people use it every day to build their vocabulary and keep up consistent practice.

iTalki is a tutoring marketplace where you book live video sessions with real human tutors. You choose your tutor based on their profile, read reviews from other learners, and book lessons around your schedule. Every session is a real conversation with a real person who can answer your questions, correct your mistakes, and push you at exactly the right level.

The core difference is simulation versus reality. Duolingo is excellent at building vocabulary and keeping you in a daily learning habit. iTalki is the fastest path to real conversational ability because you actually talk to people. Most serious English learners eventually use both: Duolingo for daily practice and iTalki for conversation coaching.

Detailed Analysis

Speaking Practice

iTalki: Real Conversations With Real Tutors

Speaking practice on iTalki means booking a lesson and talking to a real person over video. You choose your tutor, set your goals, and have a genuine conversation. Your tutor hears exactly how you sound, notices patterns in your mistakes, and explains why certain phrases work in one context but not another.

The key advantage is feedback that only a human can give. Real conversations are unpredictable. You have to think on your feet, ask follow-up questions, and handle topics you did not prepare for. This is exactly what real communication requires, and iTalki is the only platform of the two that builds this ability.

Duolingo: Gamified Speaking Drills

Duolingo has expanded its speaking features significantly. The AI role-play feature lets you have spoken conversations with virtual characters in various scenarios. You also get pronunciation scoring through voice recognition, which gives you feedback on individual words and phrases.

However, Duolingo speaking practice is still fundamentally different from real conversation. The AI characters follow scripts. They do not ask you unexpected questions, do not laugh at your jokes, and cannot explain why a phrase sounds unnatural in a business meeting. For building the muscle memory of real speaking, nothing beats talking to a real person.

Where Duolingo really shines is keeping you consistent. Its streaks, leagues, and daily goals create a habit that most learners cannot maintain with iTalki alone. Many learners use Duolingo to build vocabulary and listening comprehension, then use iTalki for actual speaking practice.

Price and Value

Duolingo is free to use with a surprisingly generous free tier. You can make significant progress in vocabulary and grammar without spending a cent. The Super subscription at $12.99 per month removes ads and unlocks unlimited hearts and full story content, but it is entirely optional.

iTalki lessons start at $5 per lesson with community tutors. Professional tutors charge more, typically $15 to $50 per session. You pay per lesson rather than monthly, so there is no automatic charge unless you book a session. For learners who want one or two focused conversation sessions per week, iTalki is excellent value. For pure cost efficiency on daily vocabulary building, Duolingo is unmatched.

The best value strategy is to use both: Duolingo free for daily habit building and vocabulary, then one or two iTalki sessions per week for speaking practice. Together they cost less than many subscription alternatives while delivering better results.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is iTalki better than Duolingo for learning to speak English?

Yes, iTalki is significantly better for developing real speaking ability. It connects you with real human tutors for live video conversations where you get instant feedback on your pronunciation, grammar, and natural word choice. Duolingo has an AI role-play feature, but it cannot answer your questions, adapt to your mistakes in real time, or give you the experience of talking to a real person. If your goal is to speak English confidently, iTalki is the clear winner.

Can Duolingo replace iTalki?

Duolingo cannot fully replace iTalki because it does not offer live conversation practice. Duolingo is excellent for building vocabulary and grammar through daily practice, but it does not develop your ability to hold real conversations. Most learners who want to speak fluently eventually need live practice with a human tutor, which is exactly what iTalki provides.

Should I use both Duolingo and iTalki together?

Using both together is one of the most effective learning strategies available. Duolingo builds your vocabulary and grammar foundation through daily, bite-sized lessons. iTalki then lets you put everything you have learned into practice with a real tutor. Many top language learners use this exact combination: Duolingo for daily practice and iTalki for weekly conversation sessions.

Which is cheaper, Duolingo or iTalki?

Duolingo is free to use with a very generous free tier. The Super subscription costs $12.99 per month for unlimited hearts and full access. iTalki lessons start at $5 with community tutors, paid per lesson. For pure cost efficiency on building vocabulary and grammar, Duolingo wins. For speaking practice value, iTalki pay-per-lesson is excellent because every session is a real conversation.

Does Duolingo help with English speaking?

Duolingo has improved its speaking features and now includes AI-powered role-play conversations. These let you speak to an AI character and get pronunciation feedback. However, this is still simulation rather than real conversation. For developing the ability to speak confidently with real people, nothing beats iTalki where you talk to a human who can respond unpredictably and adapt to your level.

Which Should You Choose?

Choose iTalki if:

  • -Your main goal is to speak English confidently
  • -You want instant feedback from a real human
  • -You are preparing for a test, interview, or travel
  • -You prefer flexible, conversation-based learning

Choose Duolingo if:

  • -You want to build a daily learning habit for free
  • -You are a complete beginner needing vocabulary foundation
  • -You prefer gamified, bite-sized lessons
  • -You want to supplement live tutoring with daily practice

The most effective approach is to use both: Duolingo to build vocabulary and maintain a daily habit, then iTalki for weekly live conversation practice. Together they cover everything a serious learner needs.
