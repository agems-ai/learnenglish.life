Pricing: iTalki vs Busuu

iTalki Pricing

iTalki operates as a marketplace where tutors set their own rates. Prices vary widely based on tutor experience and qualifications:

  • Community tutors: $5-$15 per hour
  • Professional tutors: $15-$40 per hour
  • Certified tutors: $40+ per hour

You purchase credits in packages, and there is no monthly subscription. You only pay for lessons you book. This makes iTalki flexible for casual learners, though costs can add up quickly if you take multiple lessons per week.

Busuu Pricing

Busuu uses a traditional subscription model with tiered pricing:

  • Basic (Free): Limited access to beginner lessons and vocabulary
  • Premium: $6.99/month (billed annually at $83.88)
  • Premium Plus: $13.99/month (billed annually at $167.88)

Premium Plus includes a certificate upon completion and access to all advanced content. The monthly plan costs $13.99/month if you prefer not to commit to an annual subscription.

Winner

Busuu wins on predictable monthly costs. However, iTalki can be cheaper if you take only one or two lessons per month with a community tutor.

Teacher Quality: iTalki vs Busuu

iTalki Tutors

iTalki has thousands of tutors worldwide. The platform allows students to filter by native speaker status, teaching certification, student reviews and ratings, and specialties like business English, exam prep, and conversation.

Before booking, you can watch tutor introduction videos, read their profiles, and check reviews from past students. This transparency helps you find the right match. However, quality varies. Some tutors are excellent; others may lack formal teaching training.

Busuu Instructors

Busuu uses a combination of pre-recorded lessons from language experts, AI-powered exercises, and community peer correction from native speakers. The courses are professionally designed by linguists and native speakers. However, you are not getting live feedback from a dedicated instructor.

Winner

iTalki wins for personalized learning with vetted human tutors. Busuu offers professionally designed courses but lacks dedicated instructor support.

Platform Features: iTalki vs Busuu

iTalki Features

  • Lesson scheduling: Book lessons at times that work for you
  • Notebook: Digital notebook where tutors leave notes and feedback
  • Community forums: Practice writing and get feedback for free
  • iTalki Planet: Free language exchange with native speakers
  • Teacher reviews: Transparent ratings and reviews from past students
  • Lesson recordings: Replay lessons to review mistakes

Busuu Features

  • Adaptive courses: Lessons adjust to your skill level and pace
  • Vocabulary flashcards: Spaced repetition system
  • Grammar exercises: Structured practice with immediate feedback
  • Speaking practice: AI-powered pronunciation checks
  • Offline mode: Download lessons for offline study
  • Certificates: Earn certificates upon course completion

Winner

Busuu wins for structured learners who want a clear path. iTalki wins for learners who want flexibility and human interaction.

Lesson Types: iTalki vs Busuu

iTalki Lessons

iTalki offers two main lesson formats:

  • Casual Conversation: Practice speaking on topics of your choice. Great for building fluency and confidence.
  • Structured Lessons: Some tutors follow a formal curriculum covering grammar, vocabulary, and specific topics.

Lessons are entirely customizable based on your needs. You can request specific materials or ask tutors to focus on areas where you struggle.

Busuu Lessons

Busuu organizes lessons into vocabulary units, grammar units, dialogues with transcripts, cultural notes, speaking exercises, and writing tasks. The lessons follow the CEFR framework (A1 to C2), so you always know your current level.

Winner

Tie. iTalki wins for conversational practice and customization. Busuu wins for structured curriculum coverage.

Cancellation Policy: iTalki vs Busuu

iTalki Cancellation

iTalki allows tutors to set their own cancellation policies. Most tutors follow these common rules:

  • More than 24 hours before: Free cancellation. Credits are refunded.
  • Less than 24 hours before: Lesson is charged in full.
  • No-show: Lesson is charged in full.

Busuu Cancellation

Busuu cancellation is straightforward. Cancel your subscription at any time, retain access until the end of your current billing period, with no cancellation fees or penalties. Request a refund within 14 days of purchase on annual plans.

Winner

Busuu wins for simplicity and user-friendliness. iTalki 24-hour rule can be strict if plans change unexpectedly.

Final Verdict: iTalki vs Busuu

iTalki and Busuu solve different problems. iTalki connects you with human tutors for live conversation practice. Busuu gives you a self-contained course app that you can study anywhere, anytime.

If your main goal is speaking fluency and you learn best through conversation, iTalki is the stronger choice. If you prefer structured self-study and want comprehensive grammar coverage at a lower cost, Busuu makes more sense.

Many learners use both: Busuu for daily grammar and vocabulary practice, iTalki for weekly conversation sessions.

Quick Recommendation

  • Budget learners with discipline: Busuu Premium
  • Speaking-focused learners: iTalki with community tutors
  • Comprehensive learners: Use both together
