Detailed Analysis

Pricing and Value

Cambly wins on unlimited access with its subscription model. For $6.50-80 per week, you get unlimited conversation practice with native speakers. no per-minute or per-lesson fees. This makes it ideal for learners who want to practice daily without watching the clock.

iTalki uses a pay-per-lesson model starting at just $5/hour for community tutors. This gives you more control over spending, but costs can add up quickly if you take multiple lessons per week. For structured, focused sessions, iTalki often provides better value.

Learning Approach

Cambly is designed for casual, pressure-free conversation practice. Log in anytime and connect with an available native speaker. Topics are flexible, and there's no curriculum to follow. It's perfect for building confidence and practicing real-world conversations.

iTalki offers a more structured approach. Book lessons with specific tutors who can help with grammar, vocabulary, pronunciation, or exam preparation. The lesson replay feature lets you review sessions later. a huge advantage for serious learners.

Tutor Quality

Both platforms feature native English speakers from the US, UK, Australia, and other English-speaking countries. iTalki separates tutors into "Community Tutors" (more casual, lower prices) and "Professional Tutors" (certified, higher prices), giving you more control over quality vs. cost.

Cambly's tutors are generally better for casual conversation but may lack the teaching qualifications available on iTalki. The quality can vary, but the unlimited access model means you can easily switch to a different tutor if one doesn't suit you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Cambly or iTalki better for beginners?

iTalki may be better for beginners who want structured guidance. Look for tutors with TEFL/CELTA certifications who specialize in teaching beginners. Cambly is better suited for intermediate learners who want to practice existing skills.

Can I use both Cambly and iTalki together?

Absolutely! Many learners use both strategically. Cambly for daily casual practice and iTalki for focused lessons on specific skills or exam prep. This combination often produces the best results.

How much does Cambly cost compared to iTalki?

Cambly costs $6.50-80 per week for unlimited access. iTalki starts at $5 per lesson (30-60 min), or roughly $5-40/hour depending on tutor selection. For heavy users, Cambly's unlimited model often works out cheaper.

Which platform has better native speakers?

Both platforms have excellent native speaker selections. Cambly tutors are primarily from the US, UK, and Australia. iTalki offers more variety including tutors from Canada, Ireland, and other English-speaking regions.
