Preply vs Lingoda: Which Platform Helps You Speak English Better?

Preply and Lingoda both connect you with English tutors, but their approaches are very different. Here is our detailed comparison to help you decide which platform fits your learning style and budget.

Quick Verdict

Preply wins on flexibility and price. If you want to choose your own tutor, set your own schedule, and pay as you go, Preply is the better choice. Lingoda wins on structure. If you want a clear curriculum, certified teachers, and a classroom environment with other students, Lingoda is worth the higher cost.

Overview

Preply is a marketplace where you browse thousands of tutors, read reviews, watch intro videos, and book lessons that fit your schedule. Each tutor sets their own price and teaches in their own style. You have full control over who you learn from and what you learn.

Lingoda operates more like an online language school. You choose a plan (group or private), select your schedule, and join classes taught by Lingoda-certified teachers. The curriculum is designed by the platform, so every teacher follows the same structure.

Both platforms help you practice English with human teachers. The right choice depends on whether you prefer freedom or structure.

Pricing Breakdown

Preply starts at $5 per session with community tutors. Professional certified tutors typically charge $15 to $40 per session. You buy credits or a package and spend them lesson by lesson. There is no minimum monthly commitment. If you book one lesson per week, your monthly cost is roughly $20 to $160 depending on tutor choice.

Lingoda uses a class credit system. Group classes start at about $15 per class, and private classes start around $25 per class. Packages range from 8 classes (about $120) to 96 classes (about $720). The more you commit upfront, the lower the per-class cost. Monthly plans range from roughly $99 to $249 depending on class type and package size.

For casual learners on a tight budget, Preply is significantly cheaper. You can take one lesson per week with a community tutor for under $25 per month. Lingoda requires a minimum commitment and costs more per class, but you get a structured curriculum and certified teachers.

Learning Experience and Teaching Style

Preply lessons are entirely driven by you and your tutor. Before your first lesson, you can tell your tutor what you want to focus on. Some learners want business English. Others want conversation practice. Your tutor adapts to your goals. This makes Preply extremely flexible, but it also means quality varies between tutors.

Lingoda classes follow a set curriculum that moves through levels A1 to C2. Every lesson has a specific topic, vocabulary set, and grammar focus. Teachers use platform-provided materials. This structure works well for learners who want a clear path from beginner to advanced without making their own lesson plans.

The key difference is autonomy versus guidance. Preply gives you freedom to learn what you want. Lingoda gives you a roadmap and holds you to it.

Teacher Quality and Qualifications

Preply divides tutors into two tiers. Community tutors are typically fluent speakers who may not have formal teaching qualifications. Professional tutors hold certifications such as TEFL, CELTA, or native-speaker credentials. You can read detailed profiles and watch intro videos before booking, which helps you choose wisely.

Lingoda only works with certified teachers. All Lingoda tutors are vetted, trained, and regularly evaluated by the platform. This consistency means you always get a qualified teacher, but you have less choice over who teaches you. You cannot pick a specific tutor, only a time slot.

If teacher qualifications are important to you, Lingoda has an advantage. If you want to choose your tutor based on personality, teaching style, or availability, Preply is better.

Scheduling and Flexibility

Preply offers maximum scheduling flexibility. You browse tutors' calendars, see their available slots, and book lessons that fit your life. Popular tutors may be booked out days in advance, but there are thousands of options. You can schedule one-off lessons or set up recurring sessions with your favorite tutor.

Lingoda has fixed class timetables. You sign up for specific class times each week. This creates a regular habit, which many learners find helpful. However, if your schedule changes or you miss a class, you lose that credit. Lingoda is less flexible than Preply.

If your schedule is unpredictable, Preply is more forgiving. If you want the discipline of fixed class times, Lingoda provides that structure.

Which Learners Suit Each Platform

Choose Preply if: You have a specific learning goal (business English, exam prep, conversation), you want to choose your tutor based on reviews and personality, you prefer pay-as-you-go with no minimum commitment, or you have a limited monthly budget.

Choose Lingoda if: You want a clear structured path from A1 to C2, you prefer a classroom environment with other students, you need an official completion certificate, you want certified teachers without vetting them yourself, or you thrive with fixed class schedules.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Preply or Lingoda better for beginners?

Lingoda is often better for beginners because its structured curriculum guides you step by step from A1 level upward. Preply is better for beginners who know exactly what they want to focus on and want to start speaking immediately with a specific tutor.

Which platform is cheaper for regular lessons?

Preply is cheaper for occasional lessons. A weekly lesson with a community tutor costs roughly $20 to $40 per month. Lingoda's minimum packages start around $99 per month. For daily or multiple-weekly lessons, both platforms become more comparable in cost.

Can I get a certificate on both platforms?

Lingoda offers official completion certificates when you finish a level. These are recognized by some employers and institutions. Preply does not offer formal certificates, though your tutor can provide progress reports if requested.

Which platform is better for business English?

Lingoda has dedicated Business English tracks that are well-structured for professional learners. Preply allows you to find tutors who specialize in business English, but you must vet them yourself. Both can work depending on your specific needs.

Can I switch between tutors easily on both platforms?

On Preply, you can book lessons with any tutor at any time. Many learners use multiple tutors for different purposes. On Lingoda, you get assigned to teachers based on class availability. You cannot choose your teacher, but you do get consistent teachers across your enrolled classes.

Verdict

Preply and Lingoda serve different learner types. Preply is the better choice if you want flexibility, lower costs, and control over your learning journey. Lingoda is the better choice if you want structure, certified teachers, and a clear curriculum that takes you from beginner to advanced.

Many learners start with Preply for its lower barrier to entry and switch to Lingoda when they want a more structured approach. Others use both, practicing with Preply tutors for conversation and attending Lingoda classes for formal study.

The most important factor is consistency. Either platform will help you improve if you stick with it. Choose the one that fits your schedule, your budget, and your preferred learning style.
