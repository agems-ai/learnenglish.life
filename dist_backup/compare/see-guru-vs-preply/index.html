Detailed Analysis

Pricing Deep Dive

Both platforms start around $5 per hour, but their pricing structures differ significantly. See Guru offers a flexible credit system where you pay for what you use. Basic AI conversations cost around $5 per hour. Premium features like detailed grammar analysis or specialized conversation partners run $15-40 per hour.

Preply uses a package model where buying more lessons upfront saves you money. New users often find tutors in the $5-10 per hour range. However, the most popular and highly-rated tutors typically charge $15-40 per hour. Preply also requires you to commit to lesson packages, which means less flexibility if your schedule changes.

For budget learners who want to practice daily, See Guru's lower baseline price and unlimited AI chat can offer better value. For those who prefer structured human lessons, Preply's package deals can work out cheaper per session.

Tutor Quality and Experience

See Guru's AI tutors use advanced AI to simulate natural conversations. They never get tired, never cancel, and can discuss any topic you choose. The AI remembers your previous conversations and adapts to your level over time.

Preply's human tutors bring real-world experience and cultural context to every lesson. They can explain nuances that AI still struggles with, answer questions about idioms and slang, and provide personalized feedback based on your specific goals. The best tutors on Preply have hundreds of positive reviews and years of teaching experience.

Neither approach is objectively better. If you want consistent practice without scheduling hassle, See Guru's AI tutors excel. If you value human connection and cultural insights, Preply's tutors may serve you better.

Learning Experience and Results

See Guru works best for learners who need to practice regularly but struggle to find time or motivation. The 24/7 availability means you can practice during lunch breaks, while commuting, or late at night. The AI is patient and never judges your mistakes. You can repeat topics as many times as you need without feeling embarrassed.

Preply delivers better results for learners who need accountability and structured progress. Having a real person expecting you at a scheduled time creates motivation that AI cannot match. Human tutors can identify patterns in your mistakes, push you out of your comfort zone, and celebrate your wins with genuine enthusiasm.

In our testing, See Guru users reported higher daily practice frequency but similar long-term progress compared to Preply users who attended weekly lessons. The best approach combines both: use See Guru for daily practice and Preply for weekly structured sessions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use both platforms together?

Yes. Many learners use See Guru for daily practice and Preply for weekly structured lessons. This combination often produces faster results than using either platform alone.

Which platform is better for beginners?

See Guru is often better for beginners because the AI is patient and you can practice at your own pace without pressure. Preply can work well too if you find an experienced tutor who specializes in beginners.

Do both platforms offer refunds?

See Guru offers refunds within 7 days for unused credits. Preply's refund policy depends on the tutor and package purchased. Always check the specific terms before buying.

Which platform has better technology?

See Guru uses more advanced AI technology for conversation practice and instant feedback. Preply focuses on matching you with qualified human tutors rather than AI features.

Final Verdict

See Guru and Preply serve different needs in the English learning market. See Guru wins for learners who want affordable, unlimited practice at any time of day. Preply wins for those who value human connection, structured curriculum, and cultural exchange.

If we had to pick one platform for most learners, we would choose See Guru for its accessibility and value. The ability to practice conversations at any time without scheduling or paying per session makes it easier to build consistent learning habits.

However, serious learners should consider using both platforms together. Start your day with See Guru for warm-up conversations, then book a weekly Preply session for deeper feedback and structured practice.
