Detailed Analysis

Curriculum and Learning Structure

Lingoda offers a structured, CEFR-aligned curriculum progressing from A1 to C2 levels. Classes are designed by language teaching professionals and follow European language standards. This formal approach feels like attending a traditional language school online.

See Guru provides a more informal tutoring experience without a structured curriculum. Lessons focus on conversation practice and can be customized to your specific needs. There's no formal progression system or course structure.

Teacher Quality

Lingoda employs certified, professional language teachers who are trained and vetted. All teachers meet specific qualification requirements, ensuring consistent quality across the platform. The Berlin-based company maintains high standards for instructor selection.

See Guru uses a more basic vetting process for tutors. Quality varies more significantly between teachers. The platform excels at connecting learners with Asian tutors who understand cultural nuances in language use.

Learning Experience

Lingoda offers both group classes (up to 5 students) and private one-on-one lessons. Classes run 24/7, accommodating learners in any timezone. The structured approach includes homework, progress tracking, and formal assessments.

See Guru provides one-on-one tutoring with flexible booking. You can choose specific tutors, view their profiles, and book lessons that fit your schedule. The informal approach allows for conversational practice tailored to your interests.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is See Guru or Lingoda better for structured learning?

Lingoda is significantly better for structured learning. It offers CEFR-aligned curriculum from A1 to C2 levels, with certified teachers and official course credits. See Guru focuses on informal tutoring and conversation practice without a formal curriculum.

Which platform is more affordable, See Guru or Lingoda?

See Guru is more affordable with lessons starting at $5/hour on a pay-per-lesson model. Lingoda uses a subscription model with sessions starting at approximately €12 each. For occasional learners, See Guru offers better value.

Can I get an English certificate from See Guru or Lingoda?

Lingoda offers official course completion certificates and credits that can count toward language qualifications. See Guru does not provide certificates or formal qualifications - it's focused on conversation practice and informal learning.

Which platform has better Asian tutor selection?

See Guru has a significantly stronger focus on Asian tutors from China, Japan, and Korea, making it the better choice if you specifically want tutors from these regions. Lingoda has good teacher availability but doesn't specialize in Asian tutors.
