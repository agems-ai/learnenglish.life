Which One Should You Choose?

Choose iTalki if...

You want total control over your spending. iTalki is the only platform here that does not force you into a subscription. It is perfect if you have a busy schedule or a tight budget.

Choose Preply if...

You want to build a long-term habit. The subscription model keeps you accountable. Preply is also excellent if you need a tutor for a specific professional goal, like passing the IELTS or preparing for a job interview.

Choose Cambly if...

You just want to talk. Cambly is great for learners who are shy and want to practice conversation in a low-pressure environment. You can log in at any time and find a tutor ready to chat.

Our Verdict

For most learners, iTalki remains the top choice due to its flexibility and massive selection of tutors. However, if you prefer a structured learning path, Preply is a very close second.
