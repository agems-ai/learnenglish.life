Frequently Asked Questions

How can I submit a platform for review?

Email us at reviews@learnenglish.life with information about the platform. Our team reviews all submissions and adds qualifying platforms to our review queue.

Can I correct information in a review?

Absolutely! If you notice outdated or incorrect information, please contact us with the specific details. We verify and update reviews regularly but appreciate user input.

How can I advertise on LearnEnglish.Life?

We offer advertising opportunities for English learning platforms and related services. Contact advertise@learnenglish.life for our media kit and rates.

How do you decide which platforms to review?

We review platforms based on popularity, user demand, and market relevance. We prioritize platforms that our readers frequently ask about or that represent significant options in the English learning market. We aim to cover a wide range of price points and learning styles.
