Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free English learning app?

Duolingo and Memrise offer the best free versions with gamified lessons, daily streaks, and core vocabulary building. However, free versions have limitations compared to paid plans.

Which app is best for improving English speaking?

For speaking practice, iTalki and Cambly offer live conversations with native speakers. ELSA Speak uses AI for pronunciation feedback. Pimsleur focuses on audio-based speaking skills.

How much do English learning apps cost?

Apps range from free (Duolingo, Memrise) to $10-50/hour for live tutoring (iTalki, Preply). Monthly subscriptions typically cost $10-70/month. Some offer lifetime access options.

Can apps replace traditional English classes?

Apps are excellent supplements but cannot fully replace structured classes or tutoring. Use apps for daily practice and vocabulary building, combined with tutor sessions for speaking practice and feedback.

Our Recommendation

For most learners, we recommend a combination approach:

  • • Daily practice: Duolingo or Memrise (free)
  • • Speaking practice: iTalki (1-2 sessions per week)
  • • Pronunciation: ELSA Speak

This combination gives you structure, conversation, and feedback. the three pillars of English mastery.
