What Is See Guru?

See Guru is an online language learning platform that connects students with freelance tutors from around the world. Founded in 2017 and headquartered in China, the platform has grown to include thousands of tutors offering lessons primarily in English, though other languages are available.

Like its competitors, See Guru operates on a marketplace model where tutors set their own prices, availability, and teaching approaches. Students can browse profiles, watch intro videos, read reviews, and book trial lessons before committing to regular sessions.

The platform has gained particular popularity in Asian markets and among learners seeking tutors from that region. It positions itself as a more personal, less crowded alternative to larger platforms.

How Does See Guru Work?

Getting started with See Guru is straightforward:

  1. Create a free account — Sign up with your email, Google, or Facebook account.
  2. Set your language goals — Choose the language you want to learn and your current level.
  3. Browse tutors — Filter by language, price range, availability, specialty, and nationality.
  4. Watch intro videos — Many tutors have video introductions to help you assess their teaching style.
  5. Book a trial lesson — Trial sessions (typically 25 minutes) let you test the fit before regular lessons.
  6. Schedule ongoing lessons — Book regular sessions that match your calendar.
  7. Track progress — Use built-in tools to review notes and monitor your improvement.

See Guru Tutor Tiers

Standard Tutors

General tutors offering conversation practice and basic instruction. Prices typically range from $5-15 per hour.

Verified Tutors

Tutors who have passed See Guru's verification process with ID checks and teaching qualifications. Prices typically range from $10-25 per hour.

Premium Tutors

Highly experienced tutors with strong track records and often specialized teaching backgrounds. Prices typically range from $20-40 per hour.

Key Features

Comprehensive Tutor Profiles

Tutor profiles include video introductions, detailed descriptions of teaching experience and specialties, hourly rates, availability calendars, and student reviews.

Flexible Scheduling

Real-time availability calendars make it relatively easy to find open slots. The platform supports various time zones.

Built-in Lesson Tools

The platform includes basic tools for lesson delivery, including screen sharing, digital whiteboard, and file sharing capabilities.

Mobile App

See Guru offers a mobile app for both iOS and Android, allowing you to manage lessons and take lessons on the go.

Our Verdict

See Guru offers a solid alternative to bigger platforms like iTalki and Preply, with particularly strong availability of Asian tutors and prices starting around $5/hour. The platform is straightforward to use, though it lacks some of the polish and features of established competitors. It's worth considering if you want affordable lessons without the crowds on larger platforms.

Best for budget-conscious learners seeking flexible, affordable 1-on-1 lessons with a personal touch.
